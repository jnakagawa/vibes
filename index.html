Nearfield

Phones create richer harmonics as people get closer.

A UWB sound installation. Each phone plays a simple tone; as two people approach, NearbyInteraction measures the distance between them to the centimeter and progressively unlocks harmonic overtones — isolated sounds becoming rich, beating textures that emerge from human connection. Pentatonic base notes mean any group of peers naturally forms a chord.

>3m silence 1.5m base note 1m octave 0.5m fifth <0.5m full chord
